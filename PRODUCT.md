# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Clinical and research professionals who administer ADI-R and ADOS-2 assessments. They work at a desk, often for long sessions, switching between scoring items and drafting narrative reports. They need speed, clarity, and low cognitive load.

## Product Purpose

Informer helps clinicians score structured assessment items and generate markdown reports in one workspace. Success means completing a session without friction: pick a test, score questions, preview the report, save and restore prior sessions.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, unobtrusive. The interface should feel like a serious tool (Notion, Linear), not a marketing site or a generic AI template.

## Anti-references

Purple shadcn defaults, stacked card grids, gradient heroes, heavy drop shadows, warm editorial palettes (Notion-adjacent but cold neutrals, not bone/cream minimalism), taste-skill landing aesthetics, decorative motion, emoji in UI.

## Design Principles

1. Familiar product patterns earn trust; do not reinvent navigation or controls for flavor.
2. One accent, used only for primary actions and selection states.
3. Hierarchy through type scale and spacing, not color blocks.
4. Every interactive control has hover, focus, active, and disabled states.
5. Empty states teach the next action, not just say nothing is here.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast and focus visibility. Respect prefers-reduced-motion. Keyboard-operable tabs and score controls.
